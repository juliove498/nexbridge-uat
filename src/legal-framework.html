Regulatory Bodies

The institutions that oversee and authorize NEXBRIDGE operations.

CNAD: National Commission for Digital Assets

El Salvador's official regulatory body responsible for granting licenses, approving issuances and certifiers, registering digital asset service providers, supervising operations, and ensuring compliance with the Digital Assets Issuance Law.

CNV: Argentina's Securities Commission

NEXBRIDGE is registered as Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP No. 117) under Argentina's Comisión Nacional de Valores (CNV), reinforcing regional presence as Argentina's digital asset regulations develop.

Legal Entity Identifier (LEI)

NEXBRIDGE holds a global LEI supervised by the Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation (GLEIF). This international identifier proves legal identity to regulators, partners, and investors while supporting vLEI adoption for additional fraud protection.

Compliance Features

Built-in safeguards that ensure every issuance meets institutional standards.

KYC / AML

Full identity verification and anti-money laundering checks aligned with FATF/GAFI standards for every participant.

Proof of Reserves

Monthly independent verification by Grant Thornton confirms that all issued tokens are fully backed by underlying assets.

Investor Protection

Assets are held in segregated custody with regulated counterparties, ensuring complete separation from operational funds.

Daily NAV Updates

Net Asset Value is calculated and published daily, providing real-time transparency on the value of each token.

Transparent Reporting

All key documents, including prospectuses and audit reports, are publicly available through CNAD's official registry.

Serial Issuer License

NEXBRIDGE holds a Serial Issuer license enabling streamlined issuance of multiple regulated digital assets under one authorization.
