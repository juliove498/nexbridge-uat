About NexBridge

Building the Future of
Bitcoin-Powered Finance

A regulated issuer building the foundation of Bitcoin-powered finance — connecting traditional capital markets with blockchain efficiency since 2023.

Our Mission

To set a new standard for transparent, regulated, and independently audited digital issuances — built with the rigor of traditional markets and the efficiency of Bitcoin infrastructure.

Our Vision

We believe innovation and regulatory certainty must work together — not in conflict — to build trust and unlock new possibilities for capital markets. Institutional-grade digital instruments, accessible across authorized markets, under clear public regulation.

Our Journey

Founded — 2023

NexBridge was founded in El Salvador to build regulated digital issuance infrastructure on Bitcoin rails, connecting traditional capital markets with blockchain efficiency.

Purpose

Expanding access to high-quality digital assets by bringing investment opportunities on-chain with robust regulatory standards and familiar traditional finance dynamics through licensed venues and regulated intermediaries.

USTBL Launch — Milestone

Launched USTBL, our first regulated digital instrument linked to U.S. Treasury Bills on Bitcoin infrastructure, proving real-world asset exposure can be delivered on-chain with public-market rigor. Reached $30M soft cap within 48 hours of launch (November 2024).

What's Next

Broadening our product suite, growing distribution through additional regulated exchanges, preparing licensed OTC Desk launch, and scaling distribution via authorized distributors and market participants.
